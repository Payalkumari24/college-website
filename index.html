About Us

The holy form of knowledge and learning, Gangotri, born from the great imagination of some wise men in ‘Maila Anchal’, appeared in the form of ‘D.S. College, Katihar’ in August 1953. This college was fortunate that it was able to receive the accumulated goodwill of generous people like Late Rambabu Singh and Late Kalicharan Yadav.
